Company Policies and Guidelines

1. Working Hours
   - Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday
   - Flexible working hours are available with manager approval
   - Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week

2. Leave Policy
   - Annual Leave: 20 days per year
   - Sick Leave: 10 days per year
   - Parental Leave: 12 weeks paid leave
   - Public Holidays: All national holidays are observed

3. Code of Conduct
   - Respect all colleagues and maintain professional behavior
   - Confidentiality of company information must be maintained
   - No discrimination or harassment will be tolerated
   - Report any concerns to HR immediately

4. IT Security
   - Use strong passwords and change them every 90 days
   - Never share login credentials
   - Report suspicious emails or security incidents
   - Use VPN when accessing company resources remotely

5. Performance Reviews
   - Conducted bi-annually in January and July
   - Self-assessment required before review meeting
   - Goals are set collaboratively with managers
   - Feedback is continuous throughout the year
